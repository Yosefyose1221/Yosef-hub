What is Aviator Forecast?

Aviator Forecast is a fun tool that shows random predictions for the popular game called Aviator. In the Aviator game, a plane takes off and the multiplier (like 1.5x, 2.7x, etc.) increases as the plane flies. You must cash out before the plane flies away, or you lose your bet!

The Aviator Forecast on this site gives simulated predictions. It uses a random number generator to show possible multipliers. It's just for entertainment â€” it doesn't predict real game results.
